Built for IT contractors placed with enterprise clients. LinkedIn Recruiter stays in your workflow. The agent handles the manual lift before your recruiter touches anything.

Where the hours go

Screening volume

Contract IT roles generate high candidate volume. Manual pre-screening eats hours before a recruiter even starts qualifying.

Work auth is make-or-break

FAANG and enterprise clients require no-sponsorship talent. Catching this late wastes the whole pipeline and damages trust.

No ranking logic

Without scoring, the best match is buried mid-list. Time goes to whoever surfaced last, not who fits best.

How the agent works

1

LinkedIn Recruiter stays your source of truth

Profiles enter from LI Recruiter exports or your ATS. No scraping, no bots — zero LinkedIn policy exposure.

2

Work authorization detected and tagged automatically ✦

US Citizen, Green Card, EAD, H1B — each profile tagged before anyone reviews it. No-sponsorship roles are filtered at this step. Every time.

3

Profiles scored against the role

Skills, location, seniority, and availability signals are scored. Weighting is configurable per role type — not one-size-fits-all.

4

Ranked shortlist delivered — recruiter starts at #1

Top candidates land in Google Sheets, your ATS, or an email digest. Open the list, start calling — no sorting, no guesswork.
